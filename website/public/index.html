Welcome to Chrono!

We are excited to have you join our community of bloggers and readers.

Our platform is designed to make blogging easy and enjoyable for everyone, whether you are a seasoned blogger or just starting out.


Here are some of the features that you can look forward to:

  1. Simple and intuitive interface: Our app is easy to use, with a clean and simple design that lets you focus on writing.
  2. Open network: Each created blog or profile is not only available on our platform but we generate a mini-website for each blog and profile ("blog" and "profile" will redirect to examples respecteviley).
  3. Global sharing: Share your blog posts not only on social media but on the world wide web.
  4. Community engagement: Connect with other bloggers and readers by commenting on posts, following other bloggers, and more.
  5. Mobile-friendly: Our app is optimised for mobile devices, so you can read blog posts on the go.

We hope that you will enjoy using our app as much as we enjoyed creating it.


If you have questions, please feel free to read FAQ page.


Happy blogging!
